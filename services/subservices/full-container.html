Ship big, save smart: Full Container Load (FCL) with Logisticness

Need to move a large shipment? Look no further than FCL (Full Container Load). With FCL, you get exclusive use of an entire container, perfect for big cargo hauls. You pay a fixed price for the container, regardless of how much space you use.

Benefits of FCL:

  • Simplified Shipping: Avoid the hassle of mixed cargo. Your goods are the only ones in the container, streamlining loading, unloading, and customs clearance.
  • Security and Control: Enjoy complete control over your shipment. There's no risk of your cargo getting lost or damaged due to mixed goods.
  • Cost-Effective for Large Shipments: For large shipments, FCL often works out cheaper per unit than other options.
