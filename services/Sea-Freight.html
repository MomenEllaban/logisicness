Full container load solution

Ship big, save smart: Full Container Load (FCL) with Logisticness


Need to move a large shipment? Look no further than FCL (Full Container Load). With FCL, you get exclusive use of an entire container, perfect for big cargo hauls. You pay a fixed price for the container, regardless of how much space you use.

Benefits of FCL:

  • Simplified Shipping: Avoid the hassle of mixed cargo. Your goods are the only ones in the container, streamlining loading, unloading, and customs clearance.
  • Security and Control: Enjoy complete control over your shipment. There's no risk of your cargo getting lost or damaged due to mixed goods.
  • Cost-Effective for Large Shipments: For large shipments, FCL often works out cheaper per unit than other options.

Logisticness: Your FCL Experts


We take the guesswork out of FCL shipping. Our logistics specialists have the expertise to navigate global regulations and ensure smooth sailing for your cargo.

  • Seamless FCL Transport: Our team handles everything, from documentation to customs clearance, guaranteeing efficient and timely delivery.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Stay informed every step of the way with our advanced tracking system. Monitor your shipment in real-time for ultimate peace of mind.

Specialised containers keep perishables fresh
Ever wondered how fresh food and medicine get shipped around the world? Special refrigerated containers, called reefer containers, are the answer. These high-tech boxes act like giant refrigerators, maintaining a constant temperature, airflow,humidity, and drainage inside. This controlled environment ensures delicate items like fruits, vegetables, and medications stay safe and last longer.
Perfect for food and medicine companies
Reefer containers are essential for businesses that deal with temperature-sensitive products. No matter how hot or cold it gets outside, the reefer container keeps the inside at the perfect temperature. This is crucial for maintaining the quality and safety of perishables.
Global demand for fresh food drives reefer container use

Wearhousing service

Warehousing: A vital stop on your product's journey
Warehousing plays a critical role in the supply chain, acting as a temporary home for your goods before they reach their final destination. It bridges the gap between shipping stages, providing a secure and organized space to store your cargo.
Protection and Organization: The final step before shipment
Warehousing ensures your products are safe and sound before they hit the road or sea. Warehouses are designed to keep your cargo protected from external threats, with items meticulously organized on pallets and bins for easy access and tracking.
Finding the perfect fit: Different warehousing options
The type of warehouse you choose depends on your specific needs. Here are two main options to consider: •
Public Warehousing: This is a cost-effective option where you rent a portion of a shared warehouse space. This is ideal if you have a smaller volume of goods or fluctuating storage requirements. The space allocation is typically based on your cargo's weight (CBM) or weight. • •
Private Warehousing: This option provides you with exclusive use of an entire warehouse. This is a good choice for businesses with high-volume inventory or specific storage requirements, such as temperature control or specialized security measures. Private warehouses are typically rented through contracts based on your specific needs and shipment volume. •
Logisticness can help you choose the right warehousing solution
No matter your storage needs, Logisticness can help you find the perfect warehouse solution. We offer a variety of options to fit your budget and specific requirements. Contact us today to discuss your warehousing needs and ensure your products get the protection they deserve.
